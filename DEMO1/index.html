Chapter Quizzes

Knowledge that actually sticks requires testing it

Passive reading fades. Active recall locks ideas in. After every chapter, five targeted questions push you to retrieve what you just learned, building long-term retention through spaced practice.

  • Five questions per chapter, context-specific
  • Instant feedback with explanation
  • Score tracked toward chapter mastery rating
  • Missed questions highlighted for review
Chapter Quiz Question 3 of 5
According to the 3rd Law of Behavior Change, what is the most effective way to make a habit more likely to occur?
A Reduce the friction required to perform the behavior
B Use willpower to override the desire to avoid it
C Schedule a fixed time each day regardless of mood
Why this is correct
The 3rd Law is "Make It Easy." Clear argues that we overestimate the importance of motivation and underestimate the role of friction reduction.
